5. CastleFile Stays True to Its Privacy and Security Promise

• The Technology Behind CastleFile: AES-GCM Encryption. CastleFile employs AES-GCM encryption, a gold-standard algorithm trusted worldwide for sensitive data. AES-GCM ensures that even if someone intercepts your encrypted file, they won’t be able to access the content without the unique cryptographic key generated from your chess moves. GCM mode adds authentication, verifying that the encrypted file hasn’t been tampered with during transmission or storage.

• Privacy at Its Core: No Key Storage. One of CastleFile’s standout features is its commitment to privacy. Unlike traditional platforms, CastleFile doesn’t store encryption keys. This means that your security is entirely in your hands, and no external entity can access your data. This approach ensures complete privacy while eliminating the risks associated with centralized key storage.
